About

Our Vision

To become a nationally respected platform that advances the rule of law, legal scholarship, and professional integrity – while ensuring that every member contributes to and benefits from a culture of excellence.

Our Mission

To establish a self sustaining legal community where practising advocates, researchers, academics, and publishers work collaboratively. The company will generate no profit for distribution; its entire revenue – derived solely from membership fees – will be reinvested into legal research, academic programmes, publications, and institutional development.

Our Foundation

The company’s business will be strictly service oriented, non profit, educational, scholarly, social, charitable, and of public utility.
