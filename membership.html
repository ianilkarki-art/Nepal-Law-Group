Become a Founder Member

We invite legally qualified individuals who share our vision of independent, ethical, and knowledge driven legal practice to join as Founder Members.

Eligibility

Legal qualifications – such as being an advocate, legal scholar, researcher, or legally trained professional – are strongly preferred, though other professionals who can contribute to the company’s mission may also be considered.

Requirements

Founder Members must pay the prescribed founder membership fee (currently nominal, set to cover only the initial registration and documentation costs). They are expected to actively participate in the first general meeting, contribute to the formulation of the company’s initial rules and code of conduct, and uphold the company’s ethical and professional standards. They must renew their membership annually as per the rules, unless they opt for lifetime membership. Founder Members also bear a duty to promote the company’s objectives and to act in a manner that does not bring the institution into disrepute.

Why Join as a Founder Member?

By becoming a Founder Member, you will help create an institution that places legal knowledge and public service above commercial profit. You will be a founder of Nepal’s first membership driven, non profit law group – an institution designed to advance the rule of law, legal scholarship, and professional integrity for generations to come.
