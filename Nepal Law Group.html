A collaborative & sustainable legal institution

Nepal Law Group is a Profit Non‑Distributing Company registered under Sections 166 & 167 of the Companies Act, 2063. We provide a modern, transparent, and collaborative legal platform through four divisions and two support departments.

मुद्दा फाँट तथा अधिवक्ता सेवा

Litigation & Advocacy

नेपाल बार काउन्सिल अनुमतिप्राप्त सदस्यहरूद्वारा अदालत उपस्थिति, कानूनी सल्लाह र मुद्दा प्रतिनिधित्व।

Court appearances, legal advisory, and representation by Bar‑licensed members.

अनुसन्धान तथा नीति

Research & Policy

सरकार, एनजीओ, अन्तर्राष्ट्रिय निकायका लागि अनुसन्धान परियोजना, नीति पत्र, कानून सुधार प्रस्ताव।

Research projects, policy papers, law reform proposals for government, NGOs, and international agencies.

तालिम तथा शिक्षा

Training & Education

सीपीडी कार्यक्रम, कर्पोरेट तालिम, सार्वजनिक कानूनी कार्यशाला तथा सेमिनार शृंखला।

CPD programs, corporate training, public legal workshops, and seminar series.

प्रकाशन

Publications

कानूनी पुस्तक, पत्रिका, अभ्यास पुस्तिका र डिजिटल सामग्री प्रकाशन।

Legal books, journals, practice manuals, newsletters and digital content.

सुशासन संरचना

Governance Structure

Full institutional framework as per Memorandum and Articles of Association.

सञ्चालक समिति

Board of Directors

५ सदस्यीय समिति (अध्यक्ष, प्रबन्ध सञ्चालकसहित ४ अन्य)। एक सदस्य एक मतको सिद्धान्तमा साधारण सभाबाट निर्वाचित।

5‑member Board (Chairman + 4 Directors including Managing Director). Elected by General Meeting on one‑member‑one‑vote basis.

प्रमुख कार्यकारी अधिकृत (CEO) तथा सचिव

CEO & Company Secretary

सीईओ दैनिक सञ्चालनको जिम्मेवार; सचिवले कानूनी अनुपालन, अभिलेख र नियामक फाइलिङ हेर्छन्।

CEO manages day‑to‑day operations; Secretary handles legal compliance, records, and regulatory filings.

प्रशासन तथा लेखा

Admin & Accounts

कार्यालय व्यवस्थापन, लेखा, कर, अनुपालन – ६०% सदस्यता शुल्क विनियोजन।

Office management, finance, tax, compliance – 60% of membership fee allocation.

लजिस्टिक्स तथा सहयोग

Logistics & Support

आईटी पूर्वाधार, पुस्तकालय, डाटाबेस, खरिद – ४०% शुल्क विनियोजन।

IT infrastructure, library, databases, procurement – 40% fee allocation.

ऐनको पूर्ण अनुपालन (धारा १६६–१६७)

हामी मुनाफा वितरण नगर्ने, सदस्यता हस्तान्तरण नहुने, र सीमित दायित्वको सिद्धान्तमा कार्य गर्दछौं।

Full compliance with Sections 166–167

We operate under non‑distribution of profit, non‑transferable membership, and limited liability principles.

प्रशासनिक खर्च सीमा २५%

Admin expense cap 25%

धारा १६७(१)(ज) बमोजिम कुनै पनि आर्थिक वर्षमा प्रशासनिक खर्च कुल खर्चको २५% भन्दा बढी हुँदैन।

Under Section 167(1)(j), administrative expenses shall not exceed 25% of total expenses in any financial year.

लाभांश वितरण निषेध

No dividend / profit distribution

सबै अधिशेष कम्पनीको पूँजी वृद्धि वा उद्देश्य प्राप्तिमा पुनः लगानी गरिन्छ। सदस्यहरूले व्यक्तिगत लाभांश पाउँदैनन्।

All surplus is reinvested into capital or objectives. Members receive no dividends or profit shares.

लाइसेन्स अनिवार्यता (मुद्दा फाँट)

Bar license mandatory for litigation

नेपाल बार काउन्सिलको अनुमतिपत्र नभएका सदस्य मुद्दा फाँटमा काम गर्न अयोग्य हुनेछन्।

Only members holding a valid Bar Council license may work in the Litigation & Advocacy Division.

सन्दर्भ शुल्क (Referral Fee) निषेध

Strict prohibition on referral fees

आचारसंहिता अनुसार मुद्दा सिफारिस बापत कुनै पारिश्रमिक वा कमिसन लिन/दिन पाइँदैन।

No member shall receive or pay any commission or compensation for referring a case, as per the Code of Conduct.

कम्पनीको प्रबन्धपत्रमा हस्ताक्षर गरी दर्ता गराउने पाँच संस्थापकहरू।

✔ धारा १६७(१)(ख) – दायित्व सीमित (लिखित स्वीकार बाहेक)
✔ धारा १६७(१)(घ) – मुनाफा वितरण निषेध
✔ धारा १६७(१)(झ) – विघटनमा सम्पत्ति नेपाल सरकारमा हस्तान्तरण

Financial Model (summary)

Annual membership fee is determined by the Board based on departmental budgets and desired surplus. Income from research, training, and publications is retained by the company as capital. Each member files personal income tax returns.

✔ Section 167(1)(a) – No share capital; only membership fees + donations/grants
✔ Section 167(1)(b) – Limited liability (unless written consent given)
✔ Section 167(1)(d) – Prohibition on profit distribution
✔ Section 167(1)(jh) – On dissolution, assets vest in Government of Nepal
