Welcome to a New Initiative for Legal Excellence in Nepal

Nepal Law Group is a proposed profit non-distributing company to be established under the Companies Act, 2006 of Nepal. Though not yet incorporated, we are building an institution that will serve as a unique confluence of legal practice, research, academics, and publications – all driven by a membership‑based, public‑interest model.

Call for Founder Members – Be a Founder

We are now inviting legally qualified individuals and professionals who share our vision to join as Founder Members. Founder Members will have the unique honour of shaping the company’s governance, policies, and strategic direction from the very beginning.

If you believe that legal practice can be independent, ethical, and knowledge‑driven – and that a collective of members can advance law beyond individual gain – we invite you to become a Founder Member today.
